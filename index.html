FAQ

Questions? We've got answers

Is my data used to train your AI models? ▾
Never. Your data belongs to you. We use our own proprietary foundation models trained on consented, licensed datasets — your tasks, notes, and meeting transcripts stay private.
Can I migrate from Linear, Asana, or Notion? ▾
Yes — we support one-click import from Linear, Asana, Trello, Notion, and Jira. Your existing tasks, tags, and assignees migrate in minutes, not days.
What happens after the 14-day trial ends? ▾
You'll be asked to choose a plan. If you don't upgrade, your workspace moves to the Starter tier — data intact, Pro features disabled. No credit card required to start.
Does Flowly work for non-engineering teams? ▾
Absolutely. Our fastest-growing segments are marketing, design, product, and operations teams. Flowly is built around natural language, not code or technical workflows.
Is there a desktop or mobile app? ▾
Yes — native apps for macOS, Windows, iOS, and Android. All apps sync in real time and work offline with full conflict resolution when you reconnect.
